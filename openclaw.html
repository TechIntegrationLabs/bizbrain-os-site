The Journey

What Happens When You Combine Them

Day 1 — Install Both

Set up BizBrain OS with the standalone installer (or npx create-bizbrain). Install OpenClaw from openclaw.ai. Connect them through your CLAUDE.md.

Week 1 — Context Builds

BizBrain captures your clients, projects, and workflows. OpenClaw starts handling simple tasks with that context — drafting emails in the right tone, scheduling with the right priorities.

Month 1 — Patterns Emerge

BizBrain knows your communication style, client preferences, and decision patterns. OpenClaw autonomously handles morning briefings, inbox triage, and status updates.

Month 6 — Full Autonomy

Your AI employee knows everything about your business and acts on it independently. Client onboarding, content publishing, reporting, scheduling — all handled while you focus on high-value work.

Year 1 — Insurmountable Advantage

15,000+ structured context records feed every action. Your AI employee is more knowledgeable about your business than most human employees. Competitors can't replicate what you've built.
