What the Follow-Up Prompt Does

A comprehensive audit and enhancement in one conversation.

◆ Brain Health Audit

Scans your entire folder structure, identifies empty folders, stale files, missing modules, and scores your Brain's health out of 100.

◆ CLAUDE.md Optimization

Reviews your AI brain config for completeness, accuracy, and missed opportunities. Suggests sections to add based on how you've been using it.

◆ New Module Recommendations

Based on your business type and usage patterns, suggests modules you haven't activated yet — with one-click setup for each.

◆ Custom Slash Commands

Analyzes your workflow patterns and creates personalized slash commands for tasks you do repeatedly.

◆ Data Enrichment

Scans for new documents, emails, or files you've added and integrates them into the right client/project folders.

◆ Upgrade Path

If you used Quick Setup, shows what you'd gain from the full system — upgrade via the standalone installer or npx create-bizbrain.
